The Board's Three Options

Option 1: CONTINUE FULL COMMITMENT

  • Extended timeline (12-14 weeks instead of 6)

Downside: Sunk costs, board confidence shaken, opportunity cost

Option 2: PIVOT TO OUTSOURCED SERVICE

Requires:

Upside: Lower capital requirement, proven vendor, quick implementation

Downside: Loss of $87K investment, higher ongoing costs, loss of competitive edge

Option 3: SCALE BACK / HYBRID APPROACH

Requires:

  • Reduce scope to pilot-only (stay at 35% automation)
  • Measure results for 6 months
  • Decide scale/kill after data proves viability
  • Budget increase: $15K (not $35K)

Upside: Lower investment, gather real-world data, reduce risk

Downside: Limited ROI, longer timeline to break-even, organizational uncertainty
