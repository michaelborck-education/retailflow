Common Questions

📦 How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery within Australia takes 5-7 business days. Express delivery (2-3 days) is available for most postcodes. Delivery times may vary during busy periods.

🔄 What's your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for items in original condition with tags attached. Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days after we receive the return.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Afterpay for installment payments.

🚫 How do I cancel my order?

If your order hasn't shipped yet, you can cancel it for a full refund. Once shipped, you'll need to refuse delivery or initiate a return. Contact support to cancel.

👕 Do you have size guides?

Yes! Each product page has a detailed size guide. If you're unsure, our team can help - just ask in live chat or email us your measurements.

🏪 Can I shop in-store instead?

Absolutely! We have 50 stores across Australia. Use our store locator to find the one nearest you. Staff can help with orders and returns in-store.
