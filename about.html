About RetailFlow

We're Australia's leading fashion and homewares retailer, with 50 stores and a growing online presence.

Our Story

Founded in 2008, RetailFlow started as a single store in Perth with a simple mission: bring quality fashion and homewares to Australian customers at fair prices.

Over 16 years, we've grown to 50 physical stores and launched a thriving e-commerce platform. Today, we serve millions of customers annually across Australia, offering carefully curated collections in women's fashion, men's fashion, and homewares.
